Overview

The HealthSherpa Quote Generator ("the Extension") is a browser extension that helps insurance agents quickly generate health insurance quotes on HealthSherpa.com. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how the Extension handles your data.

Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or personally identifiable information (PII). Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected from users
  • No browsing history is tracked or recorded
  • No analytics or telemetry data is gathered
  • No cookies are set by the Extension
  • No data is sent to any external servers or third parties

How the Extension Works

The Extension operates entirely within your browser. It interacts only with the HealthSherpa.com website to assist with generating insurance quotes. All data entered through the Extension is submitted directly to HealthSherpa.com and is subject to HealthSherpa's own privacy policy.

Local Storage

The Extension may store user preferences (such as default settings) locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the Extension.

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Permissions

The Extension requests only the minimum browser permissions necessary to function on HealthSherpa.com. No permissions are used to access data beyond what is required for the Extension's core functionality.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
